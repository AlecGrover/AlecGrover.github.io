About

Alec Grover is an up and coming software engineer and game developer from Vancouver, Canada. He completed a Bachelor of Science in Computing Science at Simon Fraser University and is chasing his dream of working in the games industry.

"Almost as far back as I can remember, games have been a core part of who I am. From my first experience when my older cousins handed me a controller while they played Halo CE, to where I am today, one thing is certain. There's a magic in games, and I want to help bring that magic to life."
